The Lost City

# Storyline
In This Game, the player is an adventurer who must search for a lost city in the Jungle.
Along the way, they encounter dangerous creatures, ancient ruins, and other obstacles.
The Goal of the game is to find yhe lost city and uncover its secrets.

# Description
A fun and inmersive text-based adventure game. Explore a world full of mystery and adventure by 
entering text commands.
